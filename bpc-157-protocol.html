BPC-157

Body Protection Compound for tissue repair

Common Research Focus
Gut Healing • Tendon/Ligament Repair • Anti-inflammatory
Overview

BPC-157 is a synthetic 15-amino acid peptide derived from a protective protein found in human gastric juice. It has demonstrated remarkable healing properties across multiple tissue types including tendons, ligaments, muscles, and the gastrointestinal tract in research models.

Known for its cytoprotective and anti-inflammatory effects, BPC-157 promotes angiogenesis and modulates inflammatory pathways, making it a versatile subject for regenerative research.
