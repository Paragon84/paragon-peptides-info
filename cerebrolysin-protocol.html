Cerebrolysin

Porcine brain-derived neurotrophic peptides

Common Research Focus
Brain Health • Neurodegeneration • Cognitive Recovery
Overview

Cerebrolysin is a mixture of low-molecular-weight peptides and amino acids derived from porcine brain tissue. It mimics the action of endogenous neurotrophic factors and has been extensively studied for neurodegenerative conditions, stroke recovery, and cognitive enhancement.

It supports neuronal survival, plasticity, and function, making it a valuable tool in brain health and neurodegeneration research.
