GHK-Cu

Regenerative copper complex for skin & repair

Common Research Focus
Skin Rejuvenation • Collagen Synthesis • Wound Healing
Overview

GHK-Cu (glycyl-L-histidyl-L-lysine copper) is a naturally occurring copper complex with potent regenerative, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties. It is widely studied for skin health, tissue remodeling, and wound healing applications.

As we age, GHK-Cu levels decline. Research shows it stimulates collagen and elastin production, promotes angiogenesis, and modulates gene expression related to repair and anti-aging.
