Lipo-C

Lipotropic complex (MIC) for fat metabolism & liver support

Common Research Focus
Weight Management • Liver Fat Reduction • Energy & Metabolism
Overview

Lipo-C (also known as MIC or Lipotropic Injection) is a compounded sterile formulation combining key lipotropic nutrients: Methionine (an essential amino acid that aids in the breakdown and transport of fats in the liver), Inositol (a vitamin-like compound that supports fat metabolism and insulin sensitivity), and Choline (an essential nutrient critical for the synthesis of phosphatidylcholine, enabling the liver to export fats as VLDL).

Often enhanced with L-Carnitine (for fatty acid transport into mitochondria) and B-vitamins, Lipo-C is researched for its ability to promote lipolysis, support hepatic fat metabolism, enhance energy production, and serve as an adjunct in weight management and metabolic health protocols.

Typical Concentration Example: Methionine 15–25 mg/mL + Inositol 50 mg/mL + Choline 50 mg/mL (+ optional L-Carnitine 50 mg/mL, B-vitamins). Always confirm exact formulation with your compounding pharmacy.
Suggested Research Protocol
1
Standard Weekly Protocol
1–2 mL IM or SC once weekly for 8–12 weeks, combined with caloric deficit diet and exercise.
2
Intensive / Loading Phase
1–2 mL 1–2x weekly for first 4–6 weeks, then taper to maintenance.
3
Combination Protocols
Frequently studied/stacked with GLP-1 receptor agonists (e.g., semaglutide, tirzepatide) for enhanced weight management outcomes.

Potential Side Effects
  • Generally well tolerated
  • Mild injection site discomfort or redness
  • Transient nausea, flushing, or lightheadedness (rare)
Lipo-C has an excellent safety profile when used at research/clinical doses. Monitor for individual sensitivities.
