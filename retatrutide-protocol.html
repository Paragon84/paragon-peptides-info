Retatrutide

Triple agonist for advanced metabolic research

Common Research Focus
Weight Loss • Insulin Sensitivity • Energy Expenditure
Overview

Retatrutide is a novel triple agonist that targets GLP-1, GIP, and glucagon receptors simultaneously. It represents the next generation of incretin-based therapeutics, demonstrating superior weight loss efficacy compared to dual agonists in research models.

By combining the satiety effects of GLP-1/GIP agonism with the energy expenditure benefits of glucagon receptor activation, retatrutide offers a unique mechanism for metabolic research.

Key Research Benefits
