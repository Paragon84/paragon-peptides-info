Selank

Synthetic tuftsin analog for anxiety & cognition

Common Research Focus
Anxiety Reduction • Cognitive Enhancement • Mood
Overview

Selank is a synthetic analog of the immunomodulatory peptide tuftsin. It exhibits strong anxiolytic effects without sedation, along with cognitive-enhancing and immunomodulatory properties. It is widely studied for anxiety, stress, and cognitive performance under pressure.

Unlike many anxiolytics, Selank does not cause sedation or dependence, making it a unique subject for research on anxiety and cognitive function.
