Overview

Semax is a synthetic heptapeptide analog of ACTH(4-10) with potent nootropic, neuroprotective, and adaptogenic properties. Widely researched in Russia for cognitive enhancement, brain health, and stress resilience.

It enhances focus, memory, and mental clarity while providing neuroprotection and supporting recovery from neurological stress.
