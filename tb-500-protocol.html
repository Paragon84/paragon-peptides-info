Healing / Recovery Research Grade

TB-500

Thymosin Beta-4 fragment for systemic healing

Common Research Focus
Muscle Recovery • Flexibility • Injury Repair
Overview

TB-500 is a synthetic version of Thymosin Beta-4, a protein that plays a key role in actin sequestration and cell migration. It promotes healing throughout the body, not just at the injury site, making it unique among research peptides.

Research shows it enhances muscle recovery, improves flexibility, reduces inflammation, and supports angiogenesis (new blood vessel formation).

Key Research Benefits
Enhanced Muscle Recovery
Accelerates repair after intense activity
Improved Flexibility

Systemic Healing

Angiogenesis Support
Promotes new blood vessel formation
